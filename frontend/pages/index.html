Planning your Vietnam trip

Vietnam is a year-round destination, and we can design the best itinerary based on your travel dates.

The best time to visit Vietnam depends on the regions you plan to explore.
  • October to November is ideal for northern Vietnam with clear skies and pleasant weather.
  • Central Vietnam (Hoi An, Hue) may experience rain during this period, but is great to visit in the other months.
  • Southern Vietnam is very comfortable from November to March.
