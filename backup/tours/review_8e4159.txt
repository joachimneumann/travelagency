Day Details
Ha Long Bay: A UNESCO site in Vietnam with emerald waters and thousands of limestone islands. A stunning natural wonder perfect for cruising

1. To Ha Long Bay.

Detail
The vehicle and tour guide will pick you up at hotels in the Old Quarter and near the Opera House for your trip to Ha Long Bay.

2. Fighting Cock Island

Detail
Hon Ga Choi (Fighting Cocks Island) is a famous symbol of Ha Long Bay. These two limestone islets resemble a pair of chickens facing each other amidst the emerald sea.

3. Sung Sot Cave

Detail
Located on Bo Hon Island, Sung Sot Cave is one of the largest and most beautiful caves in Ha Long Bay.

4. Ti Top Island

Detail
Ti Top Island is one of the most popular stops in Ha Long Bay, known for its unique crescent-shaped beach and panoramic views

5. End of the trip

Detail
The vehicle will take you back to the hotel.
