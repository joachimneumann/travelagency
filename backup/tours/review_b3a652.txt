Day 2

Day title
HaNoi city Tour

Day Details
Explore Hanoi’s landmarks: visit the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum at Ba Dinh Square and the iconic One Pillar Pagoda, a unique structure resembling a blooming lotus flower.

1. Hoa Lo Prison

Detail
A visit to Hoa Lo Prison offers a somber and deeply moving look into Vietnam's turbulent history. From its origins as a colonial prison to its role during the Vietnam War, this site serves as a testament to the human spirit's endurance and the complex layers of war and peace.

2. Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum

Detail
The Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum is a place of deep national pride and pilgrimage. As the resting place of Vietnam's greatest leader, it stands as a symbol of the country's independence and a must-visit landmark for those wishing to understand the heart of Vietnamese history.

3. Văn Miếu Quốc Tủ Giám

Detail
Step into the Temple of Literature, a sanctuary of peace and a testament to Vietnam's intellectual heritage. As the site of the country’s first university, it remains a place where students still come to pray for academic success, blending ancient history with living tradition.

4. Một cột pagoda

Detail
The One Pillar Pagoda is a masterpiece of ancient Vietnamese architecture. Floating like a lotus above a peaceful pond, this historic temple tells a story of royal gratitude and spiritual devotion, making it a must-see symbol of Hanoi's cultural heritage.

1. Hà Nội- Hạ Long bay

Detail
Visiting Ha Long Bay is a surreal experience. I was completely mesmerized by the emerald waters and the majestic limestone karsts. It felt like stepping into a different world—peaceful, grand, and timeless. It is truly a masterpiece of nature that everyone should see at least once in their life

2. Halong Bay cruise

Detail
Choosing the right Ha Long Bay cruise depends on how much time you have and the level of luxury you prefer. While most cruises follow a similar format—including kayaking, cave exploring, and onboard dining—the experience changes significantly based on the duration.

3. Lunch on the cruise ship

Detail
Lunch on a Ha Long Bay cruise is a feast for both the palate and the eyes. Savoring fresh-caught seafood while gliding through the emerald waters and emerald peaks is an essential experience that captures the true luxury of Northern Vietnam.
