Da Nang - Hue - Hoi An

Number of days: 5

Tour description

Experience 5 days of "slow living" in Da Nang. Relax on pristine beaches, explore the Marble Mountains, and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of Vietnam's most livable city.

Discover the spiritual heart of Central Vietnam at the Marble Mountains. A journey through hidden caves, ancient pagodas, and soaring viewpoints, this natural wonder perfectly blends the beauty of the landscape with the deep traditions of Vietnamese Buddhism.

Step into the mystical atmosphere of Huyen Khong Cave, where natural light beams create a sacred glow over ancient stone altars. It is a breathtaking sanctuary of light and shadows, offering a profound sense of peace and wonder hidden deep within the marble cliffs.

Step back in time in Hoi An Ancient Town, where golden walls and lantern-lit alleys tell the story of a bygone era. From its peaceful riverfront to its vibrant night markets, Hoi An offers a nostalgic charm that captures the soul of every traveler.

Day 2

Day title
Hoi An hotel - Coconut Forest  - Son Tra Peninsula - Dragon Bridge & Love - Chicken Church

Day Details
From Hoi An, explore the Coconut Forest, Son Tra Peninsula, and Da Nang’s icons: Dragon Bridge, Love Bridge, and the Chicken Church. A perfect mix of nature and culture.

1. Son Tra Peninsula

Detail
Escape to the Son Tra Peninsula, where lush mountains meet the deep blue sea. Whether you are seeking spiritual peace at the feet of the Lady Buddha or searching for rare wildlife in the jungle, Son Tra is a breathtaking sanctuary of nature and serenity

2. Linh Ung Pagoda

Detail
The Linh Ung Pagodas are the spiritual guardians of Da Nang. Whether you are gazing up at the towering Lady Buddha on the coast or finding peace among the misty peaks of Ba Na, these sacred sites offer a profound blend of architectural beauty and tranquil spirituality.

3. Dragon Bridge

Detail
The Dragon Bridge is more than just a piece of infrastructure; it is a living masterpiece that captures the dynamic spirit of Da Nang. Witnessing the golden dragon breathe fire under the night sky is an unforgettable experience that blends modern engineering with ancient legend

4. Da Nang Cathedral

Detail
Step into a piece of history at the Da Nang Cathedral, where colonial charm meets local legend. With its striking pink walls and the famous rooster crowning its spire, this cathedral offers a peaceful, colorful retreat in the heart of bustling Da Nang

5. explore the Coconut Forest

Detail
Escape to the tranquil Cam Thanh Coconut Forest, where you can glide through emerald waters in a traditional basket boat. From exciting boat performances to peaceful sunset views, it is a unique cultural adventure that perfectly complements your visit to Hoi An.

1. Bana Hil

Detail
From world-class hotels on the shores of My Khe Beach to the misty peaks of Ba Na Hills, Da Nang is a city of endless discovery. End your day at a bustling Night Market, where the local flavors and vibrant energy capture the true spirit of Central Vietnam.

2. Night Market

Detail
Experience the vibrant pulse of Vietnam at a Night Market. Whether you are searching for the perfect handmade souvenir, feasting on fresh grilled seafood, or simply soaking in the neon-lit atmosphere, these markets offer an authentic slice of local life that you won't find anywhere else

Day 4

Day title
Hotel DaNang - Lang Co - Thien Mu Pagoda - Imperial Citadel - Incense Village - Dong Ba

Day Details
Travel from Da Nang via Lang Co Bay to Hue. Visit Thien Mu Pagoda, the Imperial Citadel, the colorful Incense Village, and Dong Ba Market.

1. Lang Co bay

Detail
Lang Co Bay is a masterpiece of nature, where the mountains dip their toes into the emerald sea. Whether you are wandering along its white sandy shores or watching the sunset over the tranquil Lap An Lagoon, Lang Co offers a peaceful escape that feels worlds away from the hustle of modern life

2. Thien Mu Pagoda

Detail
As the symbol of Hue, Thien Mu Pagoda stands as a timeless guardian of the Perfume River. With its elegant tower and peaceful gardens, it offers a deep dive into the spiritual soul and royal history of Vietnam’s former imperial capital.

3. Imperial Citadel

Detail
Step through the gates of the Imperial Citadel and travel back to the era of Vietnam’s last emperors. With its majestic palaces, ornate temples, and tranquil lotus ponds, this walled fortress remains a powerful testament to the grandeur and resilience of the Nguyen Dynasty.

4. Incense Village

Detail
Immerse yourself in a world of color at the Thuy Xuan Incense Village. A feast for the senses, this historic craft village offers a rare glimpse into a centuries-old tradition, where every 'flower' of incense tells a story of Hue’s enduring cultural heritage.

5. Dong Ba Market

Detail
Experience the soul of Hue at Dong Ba Market. From the sizzling sounds of street food stalls to the delicate craftsmanship of poem conical hats, this bustling market is a sensory journey through the traditions and flavors of central Vietnam.
