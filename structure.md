Conversion strategy for “Van travels” (what to copy, what to improve)

Your audience (Western, $500+/week) is buying:

confidence (someone competent is handling logistics)

comfort + authenticity (not backpacker chaos, not cookie-cutter luxury)

time saved (decision fatigue is real)

So your hero message should be:

Custom private trips in Vietnam/Thailand/Cambodia

Designed by experts (13+ years) + local operations

Clear inclusions, no surprises (this is huge in Vietnam)
