# Ba Na Hills - Hue - Hoi An Ancient Town

## Video Concept

20-second example trip video for Asia Travel Plan, showing a warm cinematic journey through Ba Na Hills, Hue, and Hoi An Ancient Town.

## Global Style

- Format: vertical 9:16
- Mood: upbeat, cinematic, premium travel, warm natural colors
- Lighting: soft morning light in Ba Na Hills, elegant daylight in Hue, golden hour to lantern-lit evening in Hoi An
- Camera feel: realistic travel photography, shallow depth of field where suitable, clean composition, no text in image
- Tourist consistency: use the same 2-3 tourists in every scene

## Recurring Tourist Cast

Use the same group in every prompt:

Three friendly international tourists in their early 30s traveling together in Vietnam: one woman with shoulder-length dark brown hair wearing a white linen shirt and tan trousers, one European man with short dark blond hair wearing a light blue casual shirt and beige chinos, and one woman with long light brown hair wearing a coral summer dress and a small crossbody bag. Natural smiles, relaxed body language, realistic facial features, consistent clothing and appearance across all scenes.

## Nano Banana 2 Subject Continuity Workflow

1. Generate the master tourist cast image first.
2. Upload the master tourist cast image as the subject reference for every later scene.
3. If Freepik offers reference strength, face consistency, or seed controls, keep them the same across all images.
4. Keep the same clothes, faces, hairstyles, age, body type, and group size in every scene.
5. Each scene prompt below is copy-ready and includes the repeated continuity, style, and negative instructions.
6. The destination should be the visual hero. Make the location or landmark occupy most of the frame, with the tourists smaller in the lower foreground as continuity anchors.
